## Seed Pricing

All seeds cost **50% of their sell price**:
- Carrot: $25 (sells $50)
- Tomato: $40 (sells $80)
- Pumpkin: $160 (sells $320)

## Strategy

1. **Early Game**: Buy cheap seeds (Carrot, Parsnip)
2. **Mid Game**: Harvest and reinvest in better crops
3. **Late Game**: Focus on high-value crops (Melon, Pumpkin)
4. **Plan Ahead**: Buy seeds for next season if they work across seasons

## Differences from CLI

**CLI Version (P2P Trade)**:
- Trade crops/money with other players
- Requires network connection
- Real-time multiplayer

**Web Version (Market)**:
- Buy seeds from NPC market
- No network needed
- Single-player focused
